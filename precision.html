Main Features

  • Electro-Magnetic Force Compensation technology / Load Cell based technology.
  • Overload protection and Indication.
  • 15mm Bright LED display.
  • Tare function up to full Capacity.
  • Linearity from least count to Capacity.
  • Standard Bi-directional RS232C interface for Computer.
  • Accumulation of weight in memory.
  • Automatic Zero tracking.
  • Weighing in grams, carats, percentage, GSM
  • Stainless steel weighing pan
  • Stand By mode for low consumption of power.
  • Membrane key pad with tactile switches.

Specifications

  • Approximate Weight : 6 Kg.
  • Operating temperature : 5C to 45C.
  • Power Supply : A.C. 230V ;+/- 10% 50Hz.
