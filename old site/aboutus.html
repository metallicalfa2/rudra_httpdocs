INTRODUCTION

Rudra Systems is a Mumbai, India-based organization that manufactures and markets weighing scales and provides after-sales support.

Our manufacturing facility is located in the Union Territory of Silvassa, Gujarat, India. The company's products are sold and supported under the brand name - RUDRA. (Incidentally, "Rudra" in ancient Sanskrit language stands for "magnificent", "huge".)

The company's promoter brings to the organization an in-depth experience of fourteen years plus in the field of weighing scales. What started as a ONE-MAN organization is today a strong team of dedicated professionals - right from manufacturing to marketing to customer support.

The extended family of Rudra Systems includes its network of dealers, built assiduously through years of hard work. Every dealer is connected to the head office on a 24 X 7 communication link, to provide customers with anytime, anywhere support.

The company is proud to have received the prestigious ISO 9001 Certification. This certification is recognition of the quality that goes into every step of the manufacturing processes that result in the final weighing scale emerging from the factory. Quality of workmanship and stolid customer support are two pillars on which our company rests.

Our product range is as follows:

  • LABORATORY SCALES
  • INDUSTRIAL TABLE TOP

  • HERCULES TYPE (PLATFORM SCALES WITH BETTER ACCURACY AND CAPACITY)
  • FULLY ELECTRONIC WEIGHBRIDGE FROM 20TONS TO 100TONS
  • WEIGHBRIDGE CONVERSION TO ELECTRONICS
  • AUTOMATIC FILLING SYSTEMS
