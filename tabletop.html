Main Features

  • Metallic Finish.
  • Hi-tech microcontrolled based technology with fast response.
  • Bright LED Display, easy to read.
  • Accumulation of weight in memory.
  • Automatic zero tracking.
  • Low power indication.
  • Removable stainless steel weighing platter.
  • Inbuilt battery backup with SMF rechargeable battery.
  • Sample Counting Facility.
  • Kg/Ltr Conversion.

  • Power Supply: AC 230 V 10% 50 Hz
