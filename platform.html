Application

Platform scale can be used in various industries such as chemical, metal yards, textiles, agro food and dairy edible oil, wholesale grain merchants, iron and steel, plastic and packaging etc.

 

Main Features

  • Hi-tech Micro controlled based technology.
  • Overloaded Protection upto 150% and Indication.
  • Bright LED display, easy to read.
  • Linearity from least count to capacity.
  • Accumulation of weight in memory.
  • Automatic zero tracking.
  • Low power indication.
  • Stainless steel / Mild steel weighing platter.
  • In-built battery back up with SMF rechargeble battery.


Options

  • Additional Detachable Display with 6 mtr. cord.
  • RS - 232 Computer Interface
