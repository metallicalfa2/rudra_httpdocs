FAQ's
Q. What kind of scale do I need?
Ans. This depends entirely on the application, the degree of accuracy required and whether or not it is required to be legal for trade.
Q. How rugged are your scales and will they be suitable for a harsh enviroment?
Ans. Rudra Scales offers scales that are designed to be effective in the type of enviroment in which they will be used. Once we know the application we can help you determine the scale that will best suit your need.
Q. Do you have portable scales?
Ans.

We sell portable scales that can run on AC or DC power for every application from laboratory balances to portable heavy duty scales scales.
Q. Can my scale be interfaced to a printer or computer?
Ans. Many scales have an output port (RS232) for use with an external device i.e. computer, printer, remote displayboard, etc. In some cases the output port can be added as an option.
Q. How much will a scale cost?
Ans.

There are many different scales available for a variety of applications and the cost is dependent on such factors as capacity, accuracy and output devices.
Q. What warranty do scales carry?
Ans.

All digital scales carry a minimum of one year while some components, i.e. load cells and structural steel truck scale bridges, carry a more extensive warranty.
